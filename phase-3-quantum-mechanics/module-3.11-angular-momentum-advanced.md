## 1. The Angular Momentum Algebra · 角动量代数

**Definition.** Angular momentum operators satisfy [Ĵᵢ, Ĵⱼ] = iℏ εᵢⱼₖ Ĵₖ; ladder operators J± raise/lower the m quantum number. This single algebra generates rotations and fixes the allowed spectra.

**定义。** 角动量算符满足 [Ĵᵢ, Ĵⱼ] = iℏ εᵢⱼₖ Ĵₖ；梯形算符 J± 升降量子数 m。这一代数生成转动并确定允许的能谱。

## 2. Addition of Angular Momenta · 角动量合成

**Demonstration.** Combine two spin-½'s. The four product states reorganize into a **singlet** (total S = 0, antisymmetric, the state (↑↓ − ↓↑)/√2) and a **triplet** (total S = 1, symmetric, three states). **Clebsch–Gordan coefficients** are the general bookkeeping for such combinations.

**演示。** 合成两个自旋-½。四个直积态重新组织为**单态**（总 S = 0，反对称，态为 (↑↓ − ↓↑)/√2）和**三重态**（总 S = 1，对称，三个态）。**克莱布施–高登系数**是此类合成的通用记账方式。

**Application.** The **spin singlet is exactly the pairing structure of conventional Cooper pairs** (two electrons with opposite spin), and the symmetric/antisymmetric split connects to identical-particle statistics (3.5). Selection rules in spectroscopy also come from this.

**应用。** **自旋单态正是常规库珀对的配对结构**（两个自旋相反的电子），而对称/反对称分裂与全同粒子统计（3.5）相连。光谱学中的选择定则也由此而来。
