## 1. Quantization & the Photoelectric Effect · 量子化与光电效应

**Definition.** Classical physics failed for thermal radiation — the Rayleigh–Jeans law predicted an **ultraviolet catastrophe** (infinite energy at short wavelength). **Planck (1900)** rescued it by quantizing oscillator energy in units of **E = hν**. **Einstein (1905)** went further: light *itself* is made of quanta (**photons**) of energy E = hν. This explains the **photoelectric effect** — electrons are ejected from a metal only when **hν > W** (the work function), with maximum kinetic energy **KE_max = hν − W**, independent of intensity.

**定义。** 经典物理学在热辐射问题上失败了——瑞利–金斯定律预言了**紫外灾难**（短波长处能量无限大）。**普朗克（1900 年）**通过将振子能量量子化为 **E = hν** 的整数倍来挽救这一局面。**爱因斯坦（1905 年）**更进一步：光*本身*由能量为 E = hν 的量子（**光子**）构成。这解释了**光电效应**——只有当 **hν > W**（逸出功）时电子才从金属中被射出，最大动能为 **KE_max = hν − W**，与光强无关。

**Demonstration.** The stopping voltage obeys e V_stop = hν − W: a straight line versus frequency ν with slope h and intercept fixed by W. Millikan's careful measurements (1916) confirmed it and pinned down Planck's constant h — the same h everywhere in quantum physics. Brighter light ejects *more* electrons but never raises their energy; only higher frequency does. This is incomprehensible for a classical wave and decisive for the photon. (Einstein's 1921 Nobel Prize was for this, not relativity.)

**演示。** 截止电压满足 e V_stop = hν − W：以频率 ν 为横轴的一条直线，斜率为 h，截距由 W 决定。密立根的精密测量（1916 年）证实了这一点，并确定了普朗克常数 h——在量子物理中处处相同的 h。更强的光射出*更多*电子，但绝不提高它们的能量；只有更高的频率才能做到这一点。这对经典波动理论来说是无法理解的，却是光子存在的决定性证据。（爱因斯坦 1921 年的诺贝尔奖正是因此，而非相对论。）

**Application.** The photoelectric effect established **wave–particle duality** for light and made the photon real; the photon is later quantized properly as the excitation of the electromagnetic field (Modules 6.5, 8.2), and stimulated emission of these quanta (Module 3.8) gives the laser.

**应用。** 光电效应确立了光的**波粒二象性**，使光子成为实在；光子后来被严格量子化为电磁场的激发（模块 6.5、8.2），这些量子的受激辐射（模块 3.8）产生了激光。

## 2. The Bohr Atom & Matter Waves · 玻尔原子与物质波

**Definition.** **Bohr (1913)** quantized the hydrogen atom by postulating discrete orbits with angular momentum L = nℏ, giving energy levels **Eₙ = −13.6 eV / n²** and explaining the observed spectral lines. **de Broglie (1924)** proposed the converse of Einstein's idea: *matter* has a wavelength **λ = h/p**.

**定义。** **玻尔（1913 年）**通过假设角动量 L = nℏ 的离散轨道对氢原子进行量子化，得出能级 **Eₙ = −13.6 eV / n²** 并解释了观测到的谱线。**德布罗意（1924 年）**提出了爱因斯坦思想的逆命题：*物质*具有波长 **λ = h/p**。

**Demonstration.** The **Compton effect** (1923) — X-rays scattering off electrons with a wavelength shift — confirmed that photons carry momentum p = h/λ, cementing particle behaviour for light. de Broglie's matter waves were confirmed by electron diffraction (Davisson–Germer, 1927): electrons interfere like waves.

**演示。** **康普顿效应**（1923 年）——X 射线被电子散射并发生波长偏移——证实了光子携带动量 p = h/λ，巩固了光的粒子性。德布罗意的物质波被电子衍射实验（戴维孙–革末，1927 年）所证实：电子像波一样发生干涉。

**Application.** This "old quantum theory" was a patchwork of quantization rules, but its core — quantized energy, wave–particle duality, λ = h/p — is exactly what the **wave function** ψ of Module 3.1 makes precise. de Broglie's wave *is* the quantum mechanical ψ; Bohr's energies reappear exactly from the Schrödinger equation (Module 3.4).

**应用。** 这种"旧量子论"是量子化规则的拼凑，但其核心——量子化能量、波粒二象性、λ = h/p——正是模块 3.1 中**波函数** ψ 所精确化的内容。德布罗意的波*就是*量子力学的 ψ；玻尔的能级从薛定谔方程（模块 3.4）中精确重现。
