## 1. Deep Inelastic Scattering & Bjorken Scaling · 深度非弹性散射与比约肯标度

**Definition.** **Deep inelastic scattering (DIS)** fires a high-energy lepton off a proton, e + p → e + X, with a highly virtual photon (large Q² = −q²) probing distances ≪ the proton size. The cross-section is parametrized by two **structure functions** F₁(x, Q²), F₂(x, Q²), where the **Bjorken variable** x = Q²/(2p·q) ∈ (0,1).

**定义。** **深度非弹性散射(DIS)**将高能轻子打在质子上,e + p → e + X,高度虚化的光子(大 Q² = −q²)探测远小于质子尺度的距离。截面由两个**结构函数** F₁(x, Q²)、F₂(x, Q²) 参数化,其中**比约肯变量** x = Q²/(2p·q) ∈ (0,1)。

**Demonstration.** Experimentally, at large Q² the structure functions depend (almost) only on x, not Q² — **Bjorken scaling**. Scaling is the signature of **pointlike constituents**: the photon scatters elastically off free, structureless **partons**. The **Callan–Gross relation 2xF₁ = F₂** then shows the charged partons are **spin-½** — i.e. the quarks of Module 8.8.

**演示。** 实验上,在大 Q² 时结构函数(几乎)只依赖于 x 而不依赖 Q²——**比约肯标度**。标度是**点状组分**的标志:光子从自由、无结构的**部分子**上弹性散射。**卡兰–格罗斯关系 2xF₁ = F₂** 进而表明带电部分子是**自旋-½**——即模块 8.8 的夸克。

## 2. Parton Distributions & Scaling Violations · 部分子分布与标度破坏

**Definition.** The proton is described by **parton distribution functions (PDFs)** f_i(x): the probability density that parton i carries momentum fraction x. Then F₂(x) = Σ_i e_i² x f_i(x), summing over quark charges. The momentum sum rule Σ_i ∫ x f_i(x) dx reveals that quarks carry only ~50% of the proton momentum — the rest is carried by **gluons**.

**定义。** 质子由**部分子分布函数(PDF)** f_i(x) 描述:部分子 i 携带动量分数 x 的概率密度。于是 F₂(x) = Σ_i e_i² x f_i(x),对夸克电荷求和。动量求和规则 Σ_i ∫ x f_i(x) dx 揭示夸克仅携带约 50% 的质子动量——其余由**胶子**携带。

**Application.** QCD predicts that scaling is **weakly violated**: the PDFs evolve with Q² according to the **DGLAP equations**, a direct consequence of asymptotic freedom (Module 8.3). The measured logarithmic scaling violations confirm gluons and the running coupling. PDFs measured in DIS are then *used as input* for every hadron-collider prediction, including Higgs production at the LHC (Module 8.4) — DIS is the experimental foundation of perturbative QCD.

**应用。** QCD 预言标度被**弱破坏**:PDF 随 Q² 按 **DGLAP 方程** 演化,这是渐近自由(模块 8.3)的直接后果。测得的对数标度破坏证实了胶子与跑动耦合。DIS 中测得的 PDF 随后被*用作输入*,用于每一个强子对撞机预言,包括 LHC 上的希格斯产生(模块 8.4)——DIS 是微扰 QCD 的实验基础。
