## 1. Flavor Symmetry & the Eightfold Way · 味对称性与八重法

**Definition.** The six **quarks** carry electric charge $+2/3$ ($u, c, t$) or $-1/3$ ($d, s, b$) and come in three generations (Module 8.5). Because the light quarks $u, d, s$ have similar (small) masses, the strong interaction has an approximate **flavor $SU(3)$** symmetry (and an exact **isospin $SU(2)$** for $u, d$). **Hadrons** are the color-singlet bound states: **mesons** $q\bar{q}$ and **baryons** $qqq$.

**定义。** 六种**夸克**带电荷 $+2/3$($u$、$c$、$t$)或 $-1/3$($d$、$s$、$b$),分为三代(模块 8.5)。由于轻夸克 $u$、$d$、$s$ 质量相近(且小),强相互作用具有近似的**味 $SU(3)$** 对称性(以及对 $u$、$d$ 严格的**同位旋 $SU(2)$**)。**强子**是色单态束缚态:**介子** $q\bar{q}$ 与**重子** $qqq$。

**Demonstration.** Group theory organizes the hadrons. Mesons: $3 \otimes \bar{3} = 1 \oplus 8$ (the pseudoscalar/vector octets + singlet). Baryons: $3 \otimes 3 \otimes 3 = 10 \oplus 8 \oplus 8 \oplus 1$ — the **baryon octet** ($p, n, \Lambda, \Sigma, \Xi$) and **decuplet**. This is the **eightfold way** (Gell-Mann, Ne'eman). The **Gell-Mann–Okubo mass formula** relates masses within a multiplet, and the scheme famously **predicted the $\Omega^-$** ($sss$) before its discovery.

**演示。** 群论组织了强子。介子:$3 \otimes \bar{3} = 1 \oplus 8$(赝标量/矢量八重态 + 单态)。重子:$3 \otimes 3 \otimes 3 = 10 \oplus 8 \oplus 8 \oplus 1$——**重子八重态**($p$、$n$、$\Lambda$、$\Sigma$、$\Xi$)与**十重态**。这就是**八重法**(盖尔曼、内埃曼)。**盖尔曼–大久保质量公式**联系多重态内的质量,该方案著名地在 **$\Omega^-$**($sss$)被发现之前预言了它。

## 2. Color & the Statistics Problem · 色与统计问题

**Definition.** The decuplet baryon **$\Delta^{++} = u{\uparrow}u{\uparrow}u{\uparrow}$** appears to be a symmetric state of three identical spin-$\tfrac12$ fermions — violating the Pauli principle (Module 3.5). The resolution is **color**: each quark carries one of three colors, and the baryon wavefunction is **totally antisymmetric in color** (a color singlet $\epsilon^{abc} q_a q_b q_c$), restoring antisymmetry overall.

**定义。** 十重态重子 **$\Delta^{++} = u{\uparrow}u{\uparrow}u{\uparrow}$** 似乎是三个全同自旋-$\tfrac12$ 费米子的对称态——违反泡利原理(模块 3.5)。解决之道是**色**:每个夸克带三种颜色之一,重子波函数在**色空间中完全反对称**(色单态 $\epsilon^{abc} q_a q_b q_c$),从而恢复整体反对称。

**Application.** Color is the charge of **QCD** (Module 8.3); the factor of 3 from color is confirmed by the R-ratio $\sigma(e^+e^- \to \text{hadrons})/\sigma(e^+e^- \to \mu^+\mu^-)$ and the $\pi^0 \to \gamma\gamma$ rate (Module 6.9). The quark model classifies the entire hadron spectrum and is the static, low-energy face of QCD, whose dynamical, high-energy face is probed by deep inelastic scattering (Module 8.9).

**应用。** 色是 **QCD** 的荷(模块 8.3);来自色的因子 3 由 R 比值 $\sigma(e^+e^- \to \text{强子})/\sigma(e^+e^- \to \mu^+\mu^-)$ 和 $\pi^0 \to \gamma\gamma$ 率(模块 6.9)证实。夸克模型对整个强子谱进行分类,是 QCD 的静态、低能侧面,而其动态、高能侧面由深度非弹性散射探测(模块 8.9)。
