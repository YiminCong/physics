# Roadmap — 't Hooft's "How to become a GOOD Theoretical Physicist"
# 路线图 — 特·胡夫特《如何成为一名优秀的理论物理学家》

Nobel laureate **Gerard 't Hooft** (1999 Physics Prize) maintains a famous free
"lecture-notes roadmap," *How to become a GOOD Theoretical Physicist*
([goodtheorist.science](https://www.goodtheorist.science/), hosted at Utrecht University),
listing — in dependency order — every subject one should master, from elementary mathematics
to superstring theory. This page maps that roadmap onto the modules of this repository and
records how the gaps were filled.

诺贝尔奖得主 **杰拉德·特·胡夫特**(1999 年物理学奖)维护着一份著名的免费「讲义路线图」
《如何成为一名优秀的理论物理学家》([goodtheorist.science](https://www.goodtheorist.science/),
托管于乌得勒支大学),按依赖顺序列出了从初等数学到超弦理论应当掌握的每一门科目。本页将该路线图
对照到本仓库的模块,并记录缺口是如何被补齐的。

> 't Hooft's metaphor: theoretical physics is **a skyscraper** — it must stand on solid
> foundations of elementary mathematics and classical physics; only then can you add the
> higher floors (quantum theory, relativity, field theory, strings).
>
> 特·胡夫特的比喻:理论物理是**一座摩天大楼**——它必须立于初等数学与经典物理的坚实地基之上;
> 唯有如此,才能往上加盖更高的楼层(量子论、相对论、场论、弦论)。

## Gaps filled to complete the roadmap · 为补全路线图新增的内容

- **Module 0.7 — Group Theory & Lie Algebras** (advanced mathematics: groups, representations, Lie groups/algebras, $SU(2)$/$SU(3)$ — the language of symmetry and gauge theory).
- **Phase 9 — Applied & Specialized Physics**: 9.1 Electronics · 9.2 Atomic & Molecular Physics · 9.3 Nuclear Physics · 9.4 Plasma Physics.
- **Phase 10 — Strings & Quantum Gravity**: 10.1 String Theory & Superstrings · 10.2 Approaches to Quantum Gravity.

每个新增模块都与全仓库一致:中英双语、定义→演示→应用,并配套完整逐步双语推导文档。
