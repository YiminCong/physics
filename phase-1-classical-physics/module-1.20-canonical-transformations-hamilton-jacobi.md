## 1. Canonical Transformations & Generating Functions · 正则变换与生成函数

**Definition.** A **canonical transformation** is a change of phase-space coordinates $(q, p) \to (Q, P)$ that preserves the form of Hamilton's equations (Module 1.4): there exists a new Hamiltonian $K(Q, P, t)$ with $\dot{Q} = \partial K/\partial P$, $\dot{P} = -\partial K/\partial Q$. The transformation is generated by a **generating function** $F$ through the relation $p\,dq - H\,dt = P\,dQ - K\,dt + dF$; the four standard types $F_1(q, Q)$, $F_2(q, P)$, $F_3(p, Q)$, $F_4(p, P)$ are related by Legendre transforms, with $K = H + \partial F/\partial t$.

**定义。** **正则变换**是保持哈密顿方程（模块 1.4）形式不变的相空间坐标变换 $(q, p) \to (Q, P)$：存在新哈密顿量 $K(Q, P, t)$ 使得 $\dot{Q} = \partial K/\partial P$，$\dot{P} = -\partial K/\partial Q$。变换由**生成函数** $F$ 通过关系 $p\,dq - H\,dt = P\,dQ - K\,dt + dF$ 生成；四种标准类型 $F_1(q, Q)$、$F_2(q, P)$、$F_3(p, Q)$、$F_4(p, P)$ 由勒让德变换相联系，且 $K = H + \partial F/\partial t$。

**Demonstration.** The type-2 function $F_2(q, P)$ gives $p = \partial F_2/\partial q$ and $Q = \partial F_2/\partial P$. The simple choice $F_2 = qP$ yields $p = P$ and $Q = q$ — the **identity transformation** — confirming the bookkeeping is consistent. More usefully, $F_2 = \sum_k f_k(q)\, P_k$ generates an arbitrary point transformation $Q = f(q)$ of configuration space, with the momenta transforming contragrediently.

**演示。** 第二类函数 $F_2(q, P)$ 给出 $p = \partial F_2/\partial q$，$Q = \partial F_2/\partial P$。简单取 $F_2 = qP$ 得到 $p = P$，$Q = q$——即**恒等变换**——验证了记账的一致性。更有用地，$F_2 = \sum_k f_k(q)\, P_k$ 生成位形空间的任意点变换 $Q = f(q)$，动量按逆变方式变换。

**Application.** Canonical transformations are the freedom that makes Hamiltonian mechanics powerful: one seeks a transformation in which the new Hamiltonian is as simple as possible — ideally cyclic in all coordinates, so all new momenta are conserved. This strategy is the engine of Hamilton–Jacobi theory and action–angle variables below, and the bracket-preserving structure (Section 2) is exactly what survives quantization as the commutator (Module 3.3).

**应用。** 正则变换是赋予哈密顿力学威力的自由度：人们寻求一个使新哈密顿量尽可能简单的变换——理想情况下对所有坐标都是循环的，从而所有新动量守恒。这一策略是下文哈密顿–雅可比理论和作用量–角变量的引擎，而保持括号的结构（第 2 节）正是量子化后作为对易子保留下来的部分（模块 3.3）。

## 2. Hamilton–Jacobi Theory · 哈密顿–雅可比理论

**Definition.** The **Hamilton–Jacobi (HJ) equation** seeks a type-2 generating function $S(q, \alpha, t)$ — **Hamilton's principal function** — that makes $K = 0$, so the new coordinates and momenta are all constant. With $p = \partial S/\partial q$ this gives the single first-order PDE $H(q, \partial S/\partial q, t) + \partial S/\partial t = 0$. $S$ equals the classical **action** (Module 1.3). For time-independent $H$ one separates $S = W(q, \alpha) - Et$, reducing it to the time-independent HJ equation $H(q, \partial W/\partial q) = E$, where $W$ is **Hamilton's characteristic function**.

**定义。** **哈密顿–雅可比（HJ）方程**寻求一个第二类生成函数 $S(q, \alpha, t)$——**哈密顿主函数**——使 $K = 0$，从而新坐标和动量全部为常数。由 $p = \partial S/\partial q$ 得到单个一阶偏微分方程 $H(q, \partial S/\partial q, t) + \partial S/\partial t = 0$。$S$ 等于经典**作用量**（模块 1.3）。对不含时 $H$，分离 $S = W(q, \alpha) - Et$，化为不含时 HJ 方程 $H(q, \partial W/\partial q) = E$，其中 $W$ 是**哈密顿特征函数**。

**Demonstration.** Because $K = 0$, Hamilton's equations give $\dot{P} = 0$ and $\dot{Q} = 0$: the new momenta $\alpha$ are conserved constants and the new coordinates $\beta = \partial S/\partial\alpha$ are also constants. The equations $\beta = \partial S/\partial\alpha$, inverted, give $q(t)$ — the full trajectory — purely algebraically once $S$ is known. The HJ equation thus converts the dynamics into the single problem of finding a complete integral $S$ depending on $n$ constants $\alpha$.

**演示。** 由于 $K = 0$，哈密顿方程给出 $\dot{P} = 0$ 和 $\dot{Q} = 0$：新动量 $\alpha$ 是守恒常数，新坐标 $\beta = \partial S/\partial\alpha$ 也是常数。方程 $\beta = \partial S/\partial\alpha$ 反解出 $q(t)$——完整轨迹——一旦 $S$ 已知便纯代数地得到。因此 HJ 方程把动力学转化为寻找依赖 $n$ 个常数 $\alpha$ 的完全积分 $S$ 这一单一问题。

**Application.** The HJ equation is the deepest bridge between classical and quantum/wave physics: writing the Schrödinger wavefunction as $\psi = e^{iS/\hbar}$ and taking $\hbar \to 0$ reproduces the HJ equation exactly, with $S$ the phase (the eikonal of geometrical optics, Module 1.12). It is the natural language for separable systems (Kepler, Module 1.5), and it produces action–angle variables (Section 3) when the motion is bounded.

**应用。** HJ 方程是经典与量子/波动物理之间最深刻的桥梁：将薛定谔波函数写为 $\psi = e^{iS/\hbar}$ 并取 $\hbar \to 0$，精确重现 HJ 方程，其中 $S$ 是相位（几何光学的程函，模块 1.12）。它是可分离系统（开普勒，模块 1.5）的自然语言，并在运动有界时产生作用量–角变量（第 3 节）。

## 3. Action–Angle Variables & Adiabatic Invariants · 作用量–角变量与绝热不变量

**Definition.** For a 1-D system with bounded (periodic) motion, the **action variable** is the loop integral over one period $J = \oint p\,dq$ (the enclosed phase-space area, Module 2.4). Taking $J$ as the new constant momentum, the conjugate **angle variable** $\theta = \partial W/\partial J$ advances linearly: $\dot{\theta} = \partial H/\partial J \equiv \omega(J) = \text{const}$, with $\Delta\theta = 2\pi$ per period, so the **frequency** is $\nu = \omega/2\pi = \partial H/\partial J$ — obtained without solving for the detailed trajectory.

**定义。** 对作有界（周期）运动的一维系统，**作用量变量**是一个周期上的环积分 $J = \oint p\,dq$（所围相空间面积，模块 2.4）。取 $J$ 作为新的常数动量，共轭的**角变量** $\theta = \partial W/\partial J$ 线性推进：$\dot{\theta} = \partial H/\partial J \equiv \omega(J) = \text{常数}$，每周期 $\Delta\theta = 2\pi$，故**频率**为 $\nu = \omega/2\pi = \partial H/\partial J$——无需求解详细轨迹即可得到。

**Demonstration.** For the harmonic oscillator $H = p^2/2m + \tfrac12 m\omega^2 q^2$ the action evaluates to $J = \oint p\,dq = 2\pi E/\omega$, hence $E = (\omega/2\pi)J$ and the oscillation frequency $\nu = \partial E/\partial J = \omega/2\pi$ — recovering the familiar result directly from the enclosed elliptical area. When a parameter (e.g. $\omega$) varies slowly compared with the period, $J$ is an **adiabatic invariant**: $dJ/dt \to 0$, so $E/\omega = J/2\pi$ is conserved and the amplitude scales as $\omega^{-1/2}$.

**演示。** 对谐振子 $H = p^2/2m + \tfrac12 m\omega^2 q^2$，作用量计算为 $J = \oint p\,dq = 2\pi E/\omega$，故 $E = (\omega/2\pi)J$，振荡频率 $\nu = \partial E/\partial J = \omega/2\pi$——直接由所围椭圆面积重现熟悉的结果。当某参数（如 $\omega$）相对于周期缓慢变化时，$J$ 是**绝热不变量**：$dJ/dt \to 0$，故 $E/\omega = J/2\pi$ 守恒，振幅按 $\omega^{-1/2}$ 标度。

**Application.** Action–angle variables are the foundation of integrable systems and perturbation theory (the KAM theorem, the persistence of tori under small perturbations builds directly on them). Adiabatic invariants explain why a slowly shortened pendulum's energy tracks its frequency, underlie magnetic-mirror confinement of charged particles, and historically motivated the Bohr–Sommerfeld quantization rule $\oint p\,dq = nh$ — the bridge to old quantum theory and ultimately canonical quantization (Module 3.3).

**应用。** 作用量–角变量是可积系统和微扰理论的基础（KAM 定理，环面在小微扰下的持续性直接建立于其上）。绝热不变量解释了为何缓慢缩短的单摆其能量随频率变化，是带电粒子磁镜约束的基础，并在历史上启发了玻尔–索末菲量子化规则 $\oint p\,dq = nh$——通往旧量子论并最终通往正则量子化（模块 3.3）的桥梁。
