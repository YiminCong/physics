# Phase 9 — Applied & Specialized Physics
**第 9 阶段 — 应用与专门物理**

These four modules cover the applied and specialized subjects from 't Hooft's theoretical-physics roadmap: the physics of circuits and semiconductors, the quantum structure of atoms and molecules, the nuclear force and radioactive decay, and the collective plasma state. They translate the foundational formalism of Phases 1–8 into the concrete systems that dominate technology and astrophysics.

这四个模块涵盖了 't Hooft 理论物理学习路线图中的应用与专门物理主题：电路与半导体物理、原子与分子的量子结构、核力与放射性衰变，以及集体等离子体状态。它们将第 1–8 阶段的基础形式体系转化为主导技术与天体物理学的具体系统。

## Prerequisites · 预备知识

Phases 1–3 (Classical Mechanics, Electromagnetism, Quantum Mechanics) are the essential foundation. Phase 4 (Condensed Matter) supports Module 9.1; Phases 5–6 (Superconductivity, Quantum Field Theory) provide additional context but are not strictly required.

第 1–3 阶段（经典力学、电磁学、量子力学）是必要的基础。第 4 阶段（凝聚态）支持模块 9.1；第 5–6 阶段（超导、量子场论）提供额外背景，但非严格必需。

## Phase 9 Checkpoint (blank page)

1. State Kirchhoff's two laws; solve an RC circuit for the transient charge $Q(t)$; write the impedance of a series RLC circuit and find the resonance frequency.
2. Write the Shockley diode equation and explain physically why the current is exponential in voltage; describe how a BJT or FET acts as an amplifier.
3. State Hund's rules and construct the ground-state term symbol for a given electron configuration; list the electric-dipole selection rules.
4. Write the Bethe–Weizsäcker formula and explain each term; state the radioactive decay law and derive the half-life; describe the Gamow tunnelling factor for $\alpha$-decay.
5. Define the Debye length and plasma frequency; explain the physical origin of Landau damping; state the MHD frozen-in flux theorem.
6. Define the Q-value and threshold energy for a nuclear reaction; state the Breit–Wigner formula and identify the width $\Gamma$; write the rotational-band spectrum $E_J = \hbar^2 J(J+1)/2\mathcal{I}$ and explain why only even $J$ appear in an even–even nucleus; describe the Gamow peak and how it determines the thermonuclear reaction rate; distinguish the s-process from the r-process in nucleosynthesis.

**自测（空白页）**

1. 陈述基尔霍夫两定律；求 RC 电路中电荷 $Q(t)$ 的瞬态解；写出串联 RLC 电路的阻抗并求谐振频率。
2. 写出肖克利二极管方程，并从物理上解释为何电流对电压呈指数关系；描述 BJT 或 FET 如何作为放大器工作。
3. 陈述洪德规则并为给定电子构型构造基态谱项符号；列举电偶极跃迁选择规则。
4. 写出贝特–魏茨泽克公式并解释每一项；陈述放射性衰变定律并推导半衰期；描述 $\alpha$ 衰变的伽莫夫隧穿因子。
5. 定义德拜长度和等离子体频率；解释朗道阻尼的物理起源；陈述 MHD 磁通量冻结定理。
6. 定义核反应的 Q 值与阈动能；陈述布雷特–维格纳公式并说明宽度 $\Gamma$；写出转动带谱 $E_J = \hbar^2 J(J+1)/2\mathcal{I}$ 并解释为何偶–偶核中只出现偶数 $J$；描述伽莫夫峰及其如何决定热核反应速率；区分核合成中的 s 过程与 r 过程。
