## 1. Real and Reciprocal Lattices · 正格子与倒格子

**Definition.** A crystal = a **Bravais lattice** (periodic array of points) + a **basis** (atoms per point). The **reciprocal lattice** is the Fourier transform of the real lattice (Module 0.5); its primitive cell is the **(first) Brillouin zone**. Reciprocal-lattice vectors **b**ᵢ satisfy **a**ᵢ·**b**ⱼ = 2πδᵢⱼ.

**定义。** 晶体 = **布拉伐格子**（点的周期性排列）+ **基元**（每个格点处的原子）。**倒格子**是正格子的傅里叶变换（模块 0.5）；其原胞即为**（第一）布里渊区**。倒格子矢量 **b**ᵢ 满足 **a**ᵢ·**b**ⱼ = 2πδᵢⱼ。

## 2. Diffraction · 衍射

**Demonstration.** X-rays scatter strongly when the **Bragg condition** 2d sinθ = nλ is met (equivalently, when the scattering wavevector equals a reciprocal-lattice vector). This is how crystal structures are measured.

**演示。** 当满足**布拉格条件** 2d sinθ = nλ 时（等价地，当散射波矢等于某个倒格子矢量时），X 射线发生强散射。晶体结构正是通过这种方式测量的。

**Application.** Electrons in a crystal are labeled by a wavevector **k** living in the Brillouin zone; "crystal momentum" ℏ**k** is the conserved quantity. All of band structure (4.4) and the Fermi surface (4.5) are drawn in this reciprocal space.

**应用。** 晶体中的电子由布里渊区内的波矢 **k** 标记；"晶体动量" ℏ**k** 是守恒量。所有的能带结构（4.4）和费米面（4.5）都在这个倒格子空间中描绘。
