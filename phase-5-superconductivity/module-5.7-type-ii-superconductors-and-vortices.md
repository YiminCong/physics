## 1. The Mixed State · 混合态

**Definition.** Type II superconductors ($\kappa > 1/\sqrt{2}$) don't expel field entirely. Between a lower field $H_{c1}$ and an upper field $H_{c2}$ they enter a **mixed state**: magnetic field penetrates as an array of **quantized flux vortices**, each carrying one flux quantum $\Phi_0 = h/2e$ around a normal core.

**定义。** II 型超导体（$\kappa > 1/\sqrt{2}$）不能完全排斥磁场。在下临界场 $H_{c1}$ 和上临界场 $H_{c2}$ 之间，它们进入**混合态**：磁场以**量子化磁通涡旋**阵列的形式穿入，每个涡旋围绕一个正常态核心携带一个磁通量子 $\Phi_0 = h/2e$。

**Demonstration.** The vortices repel and arrange into a triangular **Abrikosov lattice**. **Flux pinning** (trapping vortices on defects) stops them from moving under a current — and moving vortices would dissipate energy — so pinning is what lets type-II materials carry **large critical currents in high fields**.

**演示。** 涡旋相互排斥并排列成三角形的**阿布里科索夫格子**。**磁通钉扎**（将涡旋俘获在缺陷上）阻止其在电流作用下运动——而运动的涡旋会耗散能量——因此钉扎正是使 II 型材料能在**高磁场中承载大临界电流**的原因。

**Application.** Every practical high-field superconducting magnet — **MRI scanners, fusion reactors, particle accelerators** — relies on pinned type-II superconductors. GL theory (5.3) predicts this entire vortex state.

**应用。** 每一种实用高场超导磁体——**核磁共振扫描仪、聚变反应堆、粒子加速器**——都依赖于钉扎的 II 型超导体。金兹堡–朗道理论（5.3）预言了整个涡旋态。

**Self-test (blank page)**

1. State the two critical fields $H_{c1}$ and $H_{c2}$ of a type-II superconductor and describe what happens in each of the three regions ($H < H_{c1}$, $H_{c1} < H < H_{c2}$, $H > H_{c2}$). How does this differ from a type-I superconductor?
2. Each Abrikosov vortex carries exactly one flux quantum $\Phi_0 = h/2e$. Using GL theory, explain why flux comes in units of $\Phi_0$ rather than some other value, and why a vortex has a normal core of radius $\sim\xi$ surrounded by circulating supercurrent decaying over $\sim\lambda$.
3. Explain the concept of flux pinning. Why do vortices dissipate energy when they move, and how does pinning on defects allow a type-II superconductor to carry a large dissipation-free current even in a high applied field?
4. Estimate $H_{c2} \sim \Phi_0/(2\pi\xi^2)$ from the condition that vortex cores (each of area $\sim\xi^2$) begin to overlap. What does this imply for designing high-field magnets — should $\xi$ be large or small?

**自测（空白页）**

1. 写出 II 型超导体的两个临界场 $H_{c1}$ 和 $H_{c2}$，并描述三个区域（$H < H_{c1}$、$H_{c1} < H < H_{c2}$、$H > H_{c2}$）各自发生什么。这与 I 型超导体有何不同？
2. 每个阿布里科索夫涡旋携带恰好一个磁通量子 $\Phi_0 = h/2e$。用 GL 理论解释为何磁通以 $\Phi_0$ 为单位出现，以及为何涡旋有一个半径约为 $\xi$ 的正常态核心，外围环流超电流在约 $\lambda$ 的尺度上衰减。
3. 解释磁通钉扎的概念。为何运动的涡旋会耗散能量？钉扎在缺陷上如何使 II 型超导体即便在高外加磁场中也能承载大的无耗散电流？
4. 从涡旋核心（每个面积约为 $\xi^2$）开始重叠的条件估算 $H_{c2} \sim \Phi_0/(2\pi\xi^2)$。这对设计高场磁体意味着什么——$\xi$ 应该大还是小？
