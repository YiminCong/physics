## 1. The Two Defining Properties · 两个决定性性质

**Definition.** Below a **critical temperature T_c**, a superconductor shows (i) exactly **zero DC electrical resistance**, and (ii) the **Meissner effect** — active expulsion of magnetic field from its interior (perfect diamagnetism). It is destroyed above a **critical field H_c** or **critical current**.

**定义。** 在**临界温度 T_c** 以下，超导体表现出：（i）精确的**直流零电阻**，以及（ii）**迈斯纳效应**——主动将磁场从其内部排出（完全抗磁性）。超过**临界磁场 H_c** 或**临界电流**时，超导性被破坏。

**Demonstration.** The Meissner effect is *more* than perfect conductivity. A merely perfect conductor would trap whatever field threaded it when cooled; a superconductor **expels** the field regardless of cooling history (the field-cooled experiment). This distinction is the experimental heart of the subject.

**演示。** 迈斯纳效应*不仅仅*是完美导电性。一个仅仅是完美导体的材料会在冷却时将穿过它的磁场困住；而超导体则**无论冷却历史如何**都会排出磁场（场冷实验）。这一区别是该领域实验研究的核心。

**Application.** Any theory must reproduce these facts. The **isotope effect** — T_c ∝ M^{−½} in the ionic mass — is the crucial clue that **phonons** (Phase 4) mediate the pairing, pointing the way to BCS.

**应用。** 任何理论都必须重现这些事实。**同位素效应**——T_c ∝ M^{−½}（M 为离子质量）——是**声子**（第 4 阶段）介导配对的关键线索，为 BCS 理论指引了方向。
