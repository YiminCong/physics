## 1. Beyond BCS · 超越 BCS

**Definition.** The **cuprates** (from 1986) superconduct above liquid-nitrogen temperature, far higher than BCS led anyone to expect. Their pairing is **d-wave** (the gap changes sign and has nodes), and the glue is likely **not** simple phonons. **Iron-based** superconductors add another family. The pairing mechanism remains **unsolved**.

**定义。** **铜氧化物**（自 1986 年起）在液氮温度以上超导，远高于 BCS 理论的预期。其配对为 **d 波**（能隙改变符号并具有节点），而"胶水"很可能**不是**简单声子。**铁基**超导体又增加了另一个家族。配对机制至今**悬而未决**。

**Demonstration.** The cuprate phase diagram is rich and strange: an antiferromagnetic parent, a mysterious **pseudogap** region, a **strange-metal** normal state, and a superconducting dome. The d-wave gap's nodes show up directly in experiments (e.g. angle-resolved photoemission).

**演示。** 铜氧化物相图丰富而奇异：反铁磁母体、神秘的**赝能隙**区域、**奇异金属**正常态，以及超导穹顶。d 波能隙的节点在实验中直接显现（例如角分辨光电子能谱）。

**Application.** This is the live research frontier, and **room-temperature superconductivity** is its holy grail. Understanding it requires the many-body machinery of Phase 6 (spin-fluctuation pairing, strong correlations).

**应用。** 这是活跃的研究前沿，**室温超导**是其圣杯。理解它需要第 6 阶段的多体理论机制（自旋涨落配对、强关联）。
